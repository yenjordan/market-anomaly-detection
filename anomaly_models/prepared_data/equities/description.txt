Strategy 3: Equities

Purpose:
- Monitor global equity markets
- Detect regional market stress
- Identify market contagion

Features:
Base Features:
- MXUS (MSCI USA Index)
- MXEU (MSCI Europe Index)
- MXJP (MSCI Japan Index)

Derived Features:
- REGIONAL_RETURNS (7-day)
- MARKET_CORRELATIONS
- REGIONAL_VOLATILITY
- RELATIVE_STRENGTH
- MARKET_BREADTH
- TREND_STRENGTH

Advantages:
- Global market coverage
- Regional risk detection
- Contagion monitoring
- Liquid markets data

Disadvantages:
- Time zone effects
- Regional policy differences
- Market structure variations
- Currency fluctuation impact
