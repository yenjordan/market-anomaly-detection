Strategy 14: Cross Asset Momentum

Purpose:
- Track momentum across asset classes
- Identify trend breakdowns
- Detect regime changes

Features:
Base Features:
- MXUS (US equity index)
- VIX (Volatility index)
- XAU BGNL (Gold price)
- USGG10YR (US 10Y yield)
- DXY (Dollar index)
- Cl1 (Crude Oil)

Derived Features:
- EQUITY_MOMENTUM (7-day returns)
- VOLATILITY_MOMENTUM
- GOLD_MOMENTUM
- YIELD_MOMENTUM
- CURRENCY_MOMENTUM
- OIL_MOMENTUM
- CROSS_ASSET_TREND_STRENGTH
- MOMENTUM_DIVERGENCE

Advantages:
- Captures trend breaks
- Shows risk rotation
- Identifies market turns
- Multi-asset perspective

Disadvantages:
- Momentum lag exists
- Whipsaw in ranges
- Different asset dynamics
- Time horizon sensitivity
