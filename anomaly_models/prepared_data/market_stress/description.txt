Strategy 11: Market Stress

Purpose:
- Monitor overall market stress
- Detect multi-asset pressure
- Identify risk regime changes

Features:
Base Features:
- VIX (Volatility index)
- MXUS (US equity index)
- USGG10YR (US 10Y yield)
- DXY (Dollar index)
- XAU BGNL (Gold price)

Derived Features:
- VOLATILITY_REGIME
- EQUITY_STRESS_SCORE
- BOND_MARKET_STRESS
- CURRENCY_PRESSURE
- SAFE_HAVEN_DEMAND
- CROSS_ASSET_STRESS
- LIQUIDITY_CONDITIONS
- RISK_APPETITE_INDEX

Advantages:
- Multi-factor approach
- Regime change detection
- Cross-asset coverage
- Leading indicator potential

Disadvantages:
- Parameter sensitivity
- Regime classification complexity
- Signal lag in transitions
- False signal risk
