Strategy 17: VIX Momentum

Purpose:
- Monitor volatility dynamics
- Detect volatility regime shifts
- Identify fear extremes

Features:
Base Features:
- VIX (Volatility Index)
- MXUS (US equity index)

Derived Features:
- VIX_7D_CHANGE
- VIX_7D_MAX
- VIX_7D_STD
- VOLATILITY_MOMENTUM
- FEAR_GAUGE_SCORE
- REGIME_CHANGE_SIGNAL
- VOLATILITY_TERM_STRUCTURE
- PANIC_INDEX

Advantages:
- Clear fear signals
- Regime shift detection
- Market sentiment gauge
- Extreme event capture

Disadvantages:
- Lagging indicator aspects
- Volatility clustering
- Mean reversion effects
- US market centric
